Speakers

We are pleased to welcome over ten speakers ready to present the latest technologies. Prepare for an inspiring extravaganza.

Chris Mills

HEAVY-METAL CODING

In this unconventional conversation, Chris Mills will be encouraging you to grab drumsticks in addition to your laptop and beat out the learning beat - both code and drums! You will learn the basics of JavaScript and CSS as well as the drum patterns of heavy metal. This marriage is made in heaven and there is no better antidote to disappointing code than knocking out seven shades of ***** on a drum kit.

ABOUT SPEAKER

Chris Mills is a senior technical writer at Mozilla where he writes documentation and demos about open source web applications, Firefox OS and related topics. He enjoys tinkering with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and other web technologies and occasionally speaks at conferences and universities.

  • @chrisdavidmills

Shay Howe

BETTER LESS BETTER: HOW RESTRICTIONS CULTIVATE GROWTH

By setting limits, we are forcing ourselves to be more productive. They help us make decisions by focusing on the problem we are trying to solve. They enhance our integrity, which provides the best experience for our users. And they help us grow - a valuable asset in times of innovation.

ABOUT SPEAKER

As a designer and front-end developer, Shai Hou has a passion for problem solving when creating creative and intuitive products. Shay specializes in interface design and development - specialties that he regularly writes and talks about.
